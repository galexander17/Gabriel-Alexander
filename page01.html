A New Era.

Inksplash Productions aims to incite powerful emotions through the art of film, and digital media, emotions that may improve the life, or a day, of another. Not only aesthetic, but information as well. In this new age, information is disseminated in countless ways: written words on crisp paper, bound by an elaborately embroidered spine and sealed with a solidly colored, smooth hard cover, fresh-off-the-shelf, books or online articles. Information is information, and its method shouldn't be restircted due to outdated traditions. The world is moving forward, and thus mankind shall progress as well - respecting, appreciating, and preserving old cultures - yet marching onward with constructive and progressive ideals and methods. A new way of life has come. A new era.
